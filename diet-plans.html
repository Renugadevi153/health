Diet Plans

Balanced Diet

Focus: A well-rounded diet including all macronutrients and micronutrients.

Includes: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats.

Best for: Overall health, maintaining weight, and balanced nutrition.

Mediterranean Diet

Focus: Heart health and longevity.

Includes: Olive oil, fish, whole grains, nuts, fresh vegetables, fruits, lean meats.

Best for: Reducing heart disease, weight loss, and inflammation.

Ketogenic (Keto) Diet

Focus: High fat, very low carbs, moderate protein.

Includes: Avocados, eggs, nuts, cheese, fatty meats, coconut oil.

Best for: Rapid weight loss, managing epilepsy, controlling blood sugar.

Paleo Diet

Focus: Eating whole, unprocessed foods like our ancestors.

Includes: Meat, fish, eggs, vegetables, nuts, seeds, fruits.

Best for: Reducing processed food intake, weight loss, and digestion improvement.

Vegan Diet

Focus: 100% plant-based, no animal products.

Includes: Fruits, vegetables, legumes, grains, nuts, plant-based milk.

Best for: Ethical eating, heart health, and reducing cholesterol.

Vegetarian Diet

Focus: Plant-based with some animal products.

Includes: Lacto-vegetarian, Ovo-vegetarian, Lacto-ovo vegetarian.

Best for: Ethical eating, heart health, and reducing cholesterol.

Low-Carb Diet

Focus: Reducing carbohydrates for weight loss and blood sugar control.

Includes: Meat, fish, eggs, leafy greens, nuts, seeds.

Best for: Diabetes management, weight loss.

Intermittent Fasting (IF)

Focus: Eating in specific time windows and fasting for certain hours.

Popular Methods: 16:8 (fast for 16 hours, eat in 8-hour window) 5:2 (eat normally for 5 days, restrict calories for 2 days)

Best for: Weight loss, improving metabolism, anti-aging.

High-Protein Diet

Focus: Increasing protein intake for muscle gain and fat loss.

Includes: Lean meats, eggs, dairy, protein shakes, nuts, seeds.

Best for: Bodybuilders, athletes, muscle maintenance.

Gluten-Free Diet

Focus: Avoiding gluten (a protein in wheat, barley, and rye).

Includes: Rice, quinoa, corn, vegetables, fruits, dairy, meat.

Best for: Celiac disease, gluten sensitivity.

DASH Diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension)

Focus: Lowering blood pressure and promoting heart health.

Includes: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains, low-fat dairy, lean proteins.

Best for: High blood pressure, heart disease prevention.

Carnivore Diet

Focus: Eating only animal-based products (zero carbs).

Includes: Meat, fish, eggs, dairy.

Best for: Weight loss, inflammation reduction.
